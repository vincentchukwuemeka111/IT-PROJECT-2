We don't refund money paid to us for our services, ensure you pick the right bicycle that you either want to buy or rent.

2. Age restrictions

If you want to rent a bicycle, you have to be at least 18 years old. As a student, bring your ID card or NIN for identification. Younger participants need to be accompained by a parent or guardian.

We care for the safety of our clients and that is why we provide helmets that are fitted right to your size, so always be careful on the road!

4. Damage

If you want to rent a bike or any cycling gear, you will be responsible for it until the time of return. The theft and any possible bicycle damage are included.

5. Returning Equipment

Return rented bicycles at least 15 minutes prior to our closing time because we normally close by 8pm.
